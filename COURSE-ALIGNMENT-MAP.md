# Course Alignment Map
## Executive Education ↔ University Unit Integration

This document maps the relationships between the three executive education courses and the two university units.

---

## Overview: The Five Courses

| Course | Duration | Audience | Core Question |
|--------|----------|----------|---------------|
| **Exec-Ed 1:** Practical AI for Professionals | 1 day | All professionals | "How do I use AI effectively?" |
| **Exec-Ed 2:** AI-Driven Business Innovation | 1 day | Executives, strategists | "Which AI projects should we fund?" |
| **Exec-Ed 3:** AI Leadership & Project Management | 1 day | Project managers, implementers | "How do I deliver this AI project?" |
| **ISYS6020:** AI in Business Strategy & Management | 13 weeks | Postgraduate students | "Why AI?" and "How to scale?" |
| **ISYS6014:** AI-Driven Knowledge Systems | 12 weeks | Postgraduate students | "How to build?" |

## Case Study Usage

| Case Study | Exec-Ed Usage | University Usage |
|------------|---------------|------------------|
| **RetailFlow** | Primary case (all 3 courses) | Bridging/transfer exercise only |
| **Cloudcore** | Not used | Primary case (both units) |

**Rationale:**
- Exec-ed uses RetailFlow (simpler B2C context) for 1-day intensive learning
- University uses Cloudcore (complex B2B context) for deep semester-long engagement
- RetailFlow appears briefly in university courses to test framework transferability

## Key Design Principles

1. **No duplication** - Each framework appears in one university unit only
2. **Strategic vs. Operational split** - Course 2 content → ISYS6020; Course 1 content → ISYS6014
3. **Course 3 split** - Strategic elements (crisis response, governance) → ISYS6020; Execution elements (scoping, decisions) → ISYS6014
4. **Case study consistency** - Cloudcore throughout university; RetailFlow for transfer only
5. **Complementary design** - Students taking both university units get comprehensive coverage without redundancy
