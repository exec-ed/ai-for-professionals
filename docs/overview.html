Course Overview

A one-day, hands-on workshop that builds practical AI skills for immediate use in your daily work.

Who Should Attend

  • Professionals in any function or industry
  • People who want to use AI but aren't sure where to start
  • Current AI users who want to get more from their tools
  • Team leads preparing their teams for AI adoption
  • Anyone curious about practical AI beyond the hype

What You'll Learn

  • How AI tools actually work (conceptual, not technical)
  • How to write prompts that get useful results
  • How to use AI for data analysis with verification
  • How to identify AI opportunities in your own role
  • When NOT to use AI (just as important)

Learning Approach

  • Bring Your Own Device — use real AI tools all day
  • 4 hands-on exercises with real workplace scenarios
  • 4 practical frameworks you'll keep using after the course
  • Work on your own tasks, not just practice scenarios
  • Leave with a personal action plan for Monday

This is a skills workshop, not a lecture.

You'll spend most of the day with AI tools open, working through real tasks. Expect to leave with practical skills you can apply immediately.

Before You Arrive

Two things to do before the workshop.

1. Set Up Your AI Accounts

Create free accounts on at least two of these platforms:

  • ChatGPT — chat.openai.com
  • Claude — claude.ai
  • Google Gemini — gemini.google.com

Free tiers are sufficient. Test that you can send a message and receive a response.

2. Read the Pre-Reading

"AI for the Rest of Us" — a 20-minute introduction covering:

  • What AI tools are and how they work
  • What AI is good at (and what it's not)
  • The AI landscape beyond chatbots
  • Privacy and security considerations
